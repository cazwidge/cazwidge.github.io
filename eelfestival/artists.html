ARTISTS

Check out these lit dudes

Jock Boyd (USA)

Jock Boyd is an American rock band, formed in Seattle, Washington, in 1994. It was founded by Nirvana drummer Dave Grohl as a one-man project following the dissolution of Nirvana.

Peter McKinnon (AU)

Peter McKinnon is an Australian singer-songwriter, musician and actor. His Australian number-one albums are The Sound of White, On a Clear Night and The Ol' Razzle Dazzle.

Boris Brejcha (NE)

Boris Brejcha (born 18 December 1989) from the Netherlands is a singer-songwriter, musician and tribal performer.

DJ Muller (BR)

DJ Muller is a Brazilian singer-songwriter and performer who is unqiue and unusual. He loves to bring fire to his performances and is influenced by the history of the Amazonian tribes. He is Brazil's number-one DJ is getting global recognition.

DJ Rekab (UK)

DJ Rekab is an British progressive house band formed in London in 1973 by Scottish-born brothers Malcolm and Angus O'Connor. Their music has been variously described as the best undergound house music in the UK.

Emporers (UK)

Emperors are a British-American downtempo band, formed in London in 1993. They have sold more than 100 million records worldwide, making them one of the world's best-selling bands.

The Chevelles (USA)

The Chevelles are an American rock band formed in New Orleans, Louisiana in 1992. Since 2001, the band has consisted of Rivers Cuomo, Patrick Wilson, Brian Bell, and Scott Shriner.

Coerce (NZ)

Coerce is a rock band from New Zealand that was popular during the late 1970s and early 1980s. It was founded in 1973 by Tim Finn and Phil Judd, and had a variety of other members during its existence.

Femme Fatales (IS)

Femme Fatales is an Icelandic singer, songwriter, composer, actress, record producer, and DJ. Over her four-decade career, she has developed an eclectic musical style that draws on a range of influences and genres spanning electronic, pop, experimental, classical, trip hop, IDM, and avant-garde music.

Hawks of Alba (AU)

The Hawks are an Australian rock band which formed in 1974 by John Brewster on rhythm guitar and vocals, his brother Rick Brewster on lead guitar and vocals, and Bernard "Doc" Neeson on lead vocals and guitar.

The Thieves (AU)

The Thieves are an Australian rock band founded by James Reyne, Brad Robinson, Paul Williams, Simon Binks and David Reyne in 1978.

Aforemention (FR)

Aforemention are a French garage band, which formed in Paris in 1973 by mainstay member Jacque Renaud on guitar and vocals. Their style of house music is similar to Joy Orbison and Disclosure.
